Auto Insurance Fraud Detection

Insurance fraud is a growing concern for auto insurance companies, costing billions each year. Detecting fraudulent claims early can help save costs and maintain trust between insurers and customers.

Common Fraudulent Activities

  • Staged accidents or intentional collisions
  • Exaggerating damage or injury claims
  • Submitting multiple claims for same damage
  • Falsifying information on applications
